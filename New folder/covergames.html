Cover Orange Games

In these games, the object is to use the environment and object to protect you orange from
deadly rain.

Title Description
Cover Orange		 Place objects on the screen to shield your orange balls from the spiky things that the passing cloud drops.
Cover Orange 2		 Cover Orange is back! Place wheels, barrels and crates on the screen to prevent that nasty cloud from harming your fruit.
Cover Orange Players Pack		 Drop objects and protect your fruit from the wrath of the evil cloud in another installment of Cover Orange. Includes a whopping 80 levels!
Cover Orange Players Pack 2		 More player-created levels of Cover Orange. Drop objects from above the playing field to protect your fruit from spiky rain.
Cover Orange Players Pack 3		 The third level pack for Cover Orange. Drop items on the stage with the goal of creating a shelter for the oranges and apples.
Cover Orange Journey Gangsters		 Thwart the cloud's nefarious plan to ruin perfectly good oranges. Drop objects from the right spots to give the helpless fruit a proper shelter.
Cover Orange Journey Knights		 Help the fruit survive the nasty rain by shielding them with various objects. Keep your eyes peeled for stars and click on them when they appear.
Cover Orange Journey Pirates		 Help the oranges continue their journey by protecting them against stormy clouds. Use various objects and bombs to create sufficient shelters.
Cover Orange Journey Space		 The oranges have finally gone where no fruit has gone before! Help them survive in space by protecting them against deadly rain and lasers.
Cover Orange Journey Wild West		 Take a trip back to the Wild West in the third episode of Cover Orange Journey. Solve puzzles to keep the vulnerable oranges safe during the storm.
