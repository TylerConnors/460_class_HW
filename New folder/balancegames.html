Balance Games

In these games, the object is to drop shapes in such a way that they stay balanced.

There is a difference between the perfect balance and imperfect balance games

Perfect Balance
- Goal is to keep everything on screen.
Imperfect Balance
- Goal is to remove all objects from screen.
